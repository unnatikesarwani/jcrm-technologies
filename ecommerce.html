Operational Flow

Typical Order Lifecycle

1) Discovery & Add to Cart
Customer finds products via search/category, adds to cart or wishlist.
2) Checkout & Payment
Checkout with selected payment method, apply coupons, and confirm order.
3) Order Processing
Order routed to vendor/warehouse, inventory reserved, invoice generated.
4) Shipping & Delivery
Shipment arranged with courier; tracking and ETAs shared with customer.
5) Post-Delivery
Returns/refunds processed, ratings & loyalty points awarded, settlements completed.
Key Integrations
Payment gateways, SMS/Push providers, courier APIs, analytics & ERP/Accounting sync.

Consumers, merchants, marketplace operators, logistics partners and enterprise buyers.
