Registration & Workflow

1) Walk-in / Online Registration
Capture demographics, ID proof, insurance, emergency contact, and initial vitals.
2) Medical/Employment History & Records
Allergies, past encounters, medications, attached documents or employment contracts and digital record storage.
3) Triage / Job Application & Interview
Assign urgency or progress candidate through ATS, schedule interviews, record scorecards and onboarding tasks.

Shift scheduling, leave approvals, multi-level workflows and calendar integration.
5) Payroll / Settlement
Process pay runs, statutory compliance, bank file export, payslip generation and audit logs.
