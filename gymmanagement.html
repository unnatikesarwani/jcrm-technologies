Features

Gym Management Modules

Complete feature set to run a fitness center efficiently — membership operations, trainer & class management, billing, nutrition, equipment, analytics and secure multi-platform access.

1. Member Management
  • • Member registration & profile creation
  • • Track membership type, start/end dates
  • • Health metrics: weight, BMI, fitness goals
  • • Attendance tracking & check-in/out
2. Trainer & Staff Management
  • • Trainer profiles & specialization
  • • Staff scheduling & shift management
  • • Performance tracking & payroll management
  • • Role-based access (Admin, Trainer, Front Desk, Accountant)
3. Workout & Training Management
  • • Personalized workout plans for members
  • • Group classes and session scheduling
  • • Progress tracking for workouts & goals
  • • Video tutorials & exercise demos
4. Subscription & Billing
  • • Membership plans (monthly, quarterly, yearly)
  • • Automated billing & reminders
  • • Discounts, promotions, and loyalty programs
  • • Payment gateway integration (Card, UPI, Wallet, COD)
5. Diet & Nutrition
  • • Custom diet plans for members
  • • Track nutrition goals & intake
  • • Integration with fitness tracking apps
6. Equipment & Facility Management
  • • Inventory tracking of gym equipment
  • • Maintenance scheduling and alerts
  • • Room/space booking for classes
7. Reporting & Analytics
  • • Membership statistics & growth trends
  • • Trainer performance reports
  • • Attendance & revenue reports
  • • Analytics for class popularity & member engagement
8. Communication & Notifications
  • • SMS/Email notifications for membership renewals
  • • Alerts for upcoming classes
  • • Feedback collection from members
9. AI & Automation Features
  • • AI-powered workout recommendations based on member goals
  • • Predictive analytics for membership churn
  • • Automated reminders for workouts, diet plans, and renewals
  • • Chatbot for member queries & support
10. Security & Compliance
  • • Role-based access control
  • • Secure data storage & backups
  • • GDPR / HIPAA compliance (if storing health info)
  • • Audit logs for actions
11. Mobile & Web Access
  • • Mobile app for members to track workouts & classes
  • • Trainer app for scheduling & progress tracking
  • • Admin web dashboard for complete management
  • • Push notifications & alerts
