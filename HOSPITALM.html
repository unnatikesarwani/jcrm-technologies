Features

Comprehensive Modules

The HMS includes clinical, administrative, and operational modules designed to reduce manual work, improve patient experience, and ensure regulatory compliance.

1. Patient Management
  • • Patient registration & profile creation with photo & IDs
  • • Track patient history & electronic medical records (EMR)
  • • Appointment scheduling & automated reminders (SMS / email)
  • • In-patient & out-patient workflows, triage & emergency case handling
2. Doctor & Staff Management
  • • Doctor profiles, specializations & working hours
  • • Staff roles, shift scheduling, attendance & leave management
  • • Doctor-patient assignment and performance tracking
3. Appointment & Scheduling
  • • Online booking & calendar management
  • • SMS/email notifications, reminders & follow-ups
  • • Walk-in and emergency appointment handling
  • • Doctor availability & slot management
4. Pharmacy & Medicine Management
  • • Medicine inventory & stock management
  • • Prescription generation & dispensing
  • • Expiry date tracking, purchase orders & supplier management
  • • Medicine pricing & billing
5. Laboratory & Testing
  • • Lab test management (blood, urine, imaging, pathology)
  • • Test booking & sample collection workflows
  • • Test result entry, validation & reporting
  • • Lab inventory (chemicals, reagents) & instrument integration
6. Billing & Invoicing
  • • Generate bills for consultation, tests, medicines & procedures
  • • Insurance claim processing & EDI/claims export
  • • Multiple payment methods (cash, card, online)
  • • Tax, discount management & automated invoice generation
7. Operation & Surgery Management
  • • Surgery scheduling & tracking with theatre allocation
  • • Operation theatre management and checklists
  • • Pre-op & post-op patient records and consent forms
  • • Anesthesia management & safety checklist
8. Ward & Bed Management
  • • Bed allocation & availability tracking (ICU, general, emergency)
  • • Patient transfer between wards & discharge planning
  • • Room charges, occupancy reports and utilization dashboards
9. Radiology & Imaging
  • • X-ray, MRI, CT, Ultrasound scheduling and management
  • • Image storage (DICOM) & digital record keeping
  • • Report generation, radiologist notes & sharing
10. Reporting & Analytics
  • • Patient statistics & health trends
  • • Revenue, billing and financial reports
  • • Staff performance & operational reports
  • • Inventory & medicine usage analytics; customizable dashboards
11. AI & Automation (Optional)
  • • Predictive analytics for patient outcomes
  • • AI-assisted diagnosis suggestions & clinical decision support
  • • Smart appointment scheduling based on priority & availability
  • • Automated reminders for follow-ups & stock alerts
12. Security & Compliance
  • • Role-based access control and least privilege
  • • Secure patient data handling (HIPAA/GDPR readiness)
  • • Audit logs for all actions, encryption & cloud backups
13. Mobile & Web Access
  • • Patient portal: appointments, bills, reports, teleconsult
  • • Doctor portal: schedules, patient history, e-prescriptions
  • • Admin dashboard for hospital management & approvals
  • • Push notifications, secure APIs & role-specific apps

Patient Onboarding

Patient Registration & Workflow

1) Walk-in / Online Registration
Capture demographics, ID proof, insurance, emergency contact, and initial vitals.
2) Medical History & EMR
Allergies, past encounters, medications, and attached documents (scans, prior reports).
3) Triage & Appointment
Assign urgency, allocate bed/clinic slot or schedule specialist consult.
4) Admission & Care Plan
In-patient admission, care plan, surgical consent, medication orders.
5) Discharge & Follow-up
Discharge summary, prescriptions, invoice & follow-up reminders for post-op / rehab.

Security note: Use encrypted transport (TLS), hashed credentials and fine-grained RBAC. Enable audit trails for sensitive record access.
